About Me

Hey there! I'm Sandro Qatamadze—a passionate Fullstack, Mobile, and Arduino Developer, as well as a Narrative Designer. This website is your gateway to exploring my projects and diving deeper into my career journey. My adventure in the world of programming began at the age of 13, when curiosity led me to wonder how the websites and mobile apps I used daily were built. My first steps involved creating simple web pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. But I quickly realized that I wanted to push my skills further. That’s when I joined TBC Bank and Geolab, where I honed my expertise in mobile development and frontend technologies. These experiences opened the door to working on exciting projects for TBC Tech School and Geolab. Later, I explored the world of chatbots at Explorium, developing them for my personal website and the AnimaChatbotics platform. This journey greatly expanded my skills and perspectives. Additionally, I enhanced my expertise in cybersecurity and emerging technologies at IliaUni’s Cyber Lab—UniLab. Currently, I'm studying at the Chiatura Multifunctional Center, where I’m diving deep into robotics, IT networks, and artificial intelligence through the GENAIEDU project. In 2024, I took on a major challenge—competing in the European Robotics Championship, a milestone in my journey. Alongside that, I’m also a student at Skillwill’s Neo University, specializing in Python development.
